### Attributes `assets`
* `viewable_id`
* `viewable_type`: What the asset is of, variant or else
* `attachment_width`
* `attachment_height`
* `attachment_file_size`
* `position`
* `attachment_content_type` e.g. "image/png", "image/jpeg"
* `attachment_file_name`
* `type` "Image" type is most common
* `attachment_updated_at`
* `alt`
